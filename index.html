üí° Frequently Asked Questions
What are the technical requirements for videos?
Videos should be recorded in 9:16 portrait format, with a resolution of at least 1080x1920 pixels. Lighting should be even and bright, and audio must be clear and audible. Avoid background noise and use an external microphone when possible.
How long should my video be?
The optimal video length for TikTok is between 15 and 60 seconds. It's important that the first 3 seconds are particularly engaging to capture viewers' attention. Keep your content concise and to the point.
What should I consider for lighting?
Use natural light or a ring light for even illumination. Position the light source in front of you (not behind you) at eye level. Avoid harsh shadows and overly bright light. Test different positions to achieve the best result.
How can I improve my videos?
Use our Quick-Check list before each upload. Focus on authentic presentation, clear image quality, good audio, and an engaging story. Analyze successful videos in your niche and learn from other creators. Stay consistent and authentic!
